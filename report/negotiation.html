Playbook

Negotiation Strategies

1. Monetize Carrying Costs

Request seller-paid HOA credits (3–6 months prepaid) or closing cost credits. The #2805 listing disclosed a $5,009 one-time capital improvement contribution — these are negotiable.

2. Anchor on PPSF + HOA-Adjusted Payment

Don't just compare sticker prices. Show the total monthly cost vs. a Vista or Cosmopolitan unit. The Ritz HOA wipes out its price advantage.

3. Target Time-on-Market Asymmetry

Fresh listings (days old) have less leverage. Units that have sat for weeks or months have maximum seller motivation. Check listing dates before offering.

4. Demand Full Disclosure

Request: HOA financials, reserve study, status of $7.8M inclusionary fee, master insurance policy, pending assessments, and delinquency rates. Any gaps are negotiation ammunition.
